What is the commonest presentation of an abdominal aortic aneurysm?

Feedback

Abdominal aortic aneurysm most often presents as an incidental finding on abdominal examination, XR or U/S.
Can also present with:
1. Local Symptoms - vague abdominal or back pain.
2. Distant symptoms- embolisation.
3. Collapse due to rupture.
